## Shopping Strategy

### Before January 1:
1. Do a big pantry stock-up (all canned goods, dry goods, spices)
2. Buy Week 1 fresh produce

### Each Week:
1. Check what you have left from last week
2. Buy only the fresh produce and any pantry items you've run out of
3. Plan to use up perishables before they go bad

### Money-Saving Tips:
- Buy canned goods in bulk (Costco, Amazon)
- Buy raw cashews in bulk - you'll use a LOT
- Check ethnic grocery stores for cheaper spices
- Frozen fruit is often cheaper than fresh for smoothies
- Dried lentils and beans are cheaper than canned (but canned saves time)

## Soy-Free Reminder

Always check labels! These items sometimes contain soy:
- Vegetable broth (some brands)
- Curry paste (some brands use soy sauce)
- BBQ sauce (some brands)
- Vegan butter (some brands)
- Bread products

**Safe Swaps:**
- Soy sauce → Coconut aminos (always!)
- Soy milk → Oat milk, almond milk, coconut milk
- Tofu → Chickpeas, white beans
- Soy yogurt → Coconut yogurt, oat yogurt
