## Component Recipes

### Mediterranean Chickpeas (Week 1 Favorite)

**Yield:** ~4 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1.5 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp ground coriander
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1/4 cup vegetable broth or water
- Fresh parsley for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high. Add chickpeas, cook until starting to crisp, 5-7 minutes.
2. Add garlic, cook 1 minute.
3. Add spices, stir to coat.
4. Add lemon juice and broth. Cook until liquid mostly evaporates.
5. Season to taste. Top with parsley.

---

### Quick Coconut Curry (Week 2 Favorite)

**Yield:** ~5 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 medium onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 inch ginger, minced
- 2 tbsp Thai green curry paste
- 2 cans (13.5 oz) coconut milk
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained
- 1 head broccoli, cut into florets
- 1 tbsp coconut aminos
- 1 tsp coconut sugar
- Juice of 1 lime
- Fresh cilantro and basil

**Instructions:**
1. Heat oil over medium. Sauté onion 5 minutes.
2. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute.
3. Add curry paste, fry 1 minute.
4. Add coconut milk, stir well. Bring to simmer.
5. Add chickpeas and broccoli. Simmer 8-10 minutes until broccoli is tender.
6. Stir in coconut aminos, sugar, and lime juice.
7. Garnish with herbs.

### Cilantro-Lime Rice

**Yield:** ~3 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 cups long-grain white rice
- 2.25 cups water
- 1/2 tsp salt
- Juice and zest of 1 lime
- 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 tbsp olive oil

**Instructions:**
1. Cook rice with water and salt.
2. Fluff, stir in lime juice, zest, cilantro, and oil.

---

### Sheet Pan Rainbow Vegetables

**Yield:** ~5 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1 sweet potato, cubed
- 1 zucchini, sliced
- 2 bell peppers, chunked
- 1 head broccoli, florets
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp kosher salt

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
2. Toss vegetables with oil and spices.
3. Spread on sheet pan.
4. Roast 25-30 minutes, stirring halfway.

---

### Tahini Dressing

**Yield:** ~3/4 cup | **Storage:** 10 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1/4 cup tahini
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 small garlic clove, minced
- 2-3 tbsp water
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Whisk all ingredients, adding water until pourable.
2. Store in jar.

---

### Peanut Sauce

**Yield:** ~1/2 cup | **Storage:** 10 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 2 tbsp coconut aminos
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 2-3 tbsp warm water

**Instructions:**
1. Whisk all ingredients until smooth.
2. Store in jar.

---

### Quick Hummus

**Yield:** ~1.5 cups | **Storage:** 7 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained (reserve liquid)
- 3 tbsp tahini
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- Salt to taste
- 2-3 tbsp reserved chickpea liquid

**Instructions:**
1. Blend all ingredients until very smooth.
2. Serve with olive oil drizzle and paprika.

---

### Overnight Oats (2 jars)

**Per jar:**
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup oat milk
- 2 tbsp coconut yogurt
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1/2 banana, sliced
- Handful of berries

Mix, refrigerate overnight.

---

### Energy Balls (8 balls)

**Ingredients:**
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 8 dates, pitted
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- Pinch of salt

**Instructions:**
1. Blend all in food processor until mixture clumps.
2. Roll into 8 balls.
