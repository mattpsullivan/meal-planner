## Component Recipes

### Grains

#### Cilantro-Lime Rice

**Yield:** ~4 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups long-grain white rice
- 3 cups water or vegetable broth
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 2 tbsp fresh lime juice (~2 limes)
- 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Zest of 1 lime

**Instructions:**
1. Rinse rice until water runs clear.
2. Combine rice, water, and salt in pot or rice cooker. Cook until done.
3. Fluff with fork. While still hot, stir in lime juice, lime zest, olive oil, and cilantro.
4. Let cool, then transfer to airtight container.

**Reheating:** Microwave with a splash of water, covered, 1-2 minutes.

---

#### Lemon-Herb Quinoa

**Yield:** ~4 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 cups quinoa, rinsed well
- 3 cups water or vegetable broth
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
- Zest of 1 lemon
- Freshly cracked black pepper

**Instructions:**
1. **Instant Pot:** Combine quinoa, liquid, and salt. Pressure cook 1 minute, natural release 10 minutes.
2. **Stovetop:** Bring to boil, reduce to simmer, cover, cook 15 minutes. Rest 5 minutes.
3. Fluff with fork. Stir in lemon juice, zest, olive oil, parsley, and pepper.
4. Cool and store in airtight container.

### Proteins

#### Spiced Mediterranean Chickpeas

**Yield:** ~4 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 3 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 tsp cayenne (optional)
- 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1/4 cup vegetable broth or water

**Instructions:**
1. Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add chickpeas and cook, stirring occasionally, until starting to get golden, 5-7 minutes.
3. Add garlic, cook 1 minute until fragrant.
4. Add cumin, coriander, paprika, salt, pepper, and cayenne. Stir to coat, cook 1 minute.
5. Add lemon juice and broth. Stir and cook until liquid mostly evaporates, 2-3 minutes.
6. Taste and adjust seasoning. Cool and store.

**Use in:** Mediterranean bowls, Greek salad, mezze plate, scramble topping

---

#### Smoky Black Beans

**Yield:** ~4 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 3 cans (15 oz each) black beans, drained and rinsed (reserve 1/2 cup liquid)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced (or leave seeds for heat)
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 chipotle pepper in adobo, minced + 1 tbsp adobo sauce
- 1/2 cup reserved bean liquid or vegetable broth
- 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Heat oil in large pot over medium heat. Add onion, cook until soft, 5 minutes.
2. Add garlic and jalapeño, cook 1 minute.
3. Add cumin, paprika, chili powder, oregano. Stir 30 seconds.
4. Add beans, chipotle + adobo, and liquid. Stir well.
5. Simmer 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
6. Mash about 1/4 of the beans with back of spoon for creamier texture.
7. Stir in lime juice and salt. Taste and adjust.
8. Cool and store. Add fresh cilantro when serving.

**Use in:** Tacos, burrito bowls, stuffed sweet potatoes, taco salad

#### Lentil-Walnut Taco "Meat"

**Yield:** ~4 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1.5 cups brown or green lentils
- 4 cups water or vegetable broth
- 1 cup walnuts, finely chopped (pulse in food processor)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 medium yellow onion, finely diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tbsp coconut aminos
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1/4 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Cook lentils in water/broth until tender but not mushy, 20-25 minutes. Drain any excess liquid.
2. While lentils cook, toast walnuts in dry skillet until fragrant, 3-4 minutes. Set aside.
3. In same skillet, heat oil over medium. Add onion, cook 5 minutes until soft.
4. Add garlic, cook 1 minute.
5. Add tomato paste, coconut aminos, and all spices. Stir to combine.
6. Add cooked lentils and walnuts. Stir well, cook 5 minutes to meld flavors.
7. Taste and adjust seasoning. Mixture should be savory and slightly crumbly.
8. Cool and store.

**Use in:** Tacos, lettuce wraps, burrito bowls, stuffed peppers

### Sauces & Dressings

#### Cashew Tzatziki

**Yield:** ~2 cups | **Storage:** 7 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in boiling water 15+ minutes, drained
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1 clove garlic
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 medium cucumber, seeded and finely diced (about 3/4 cup)
- 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- Freshly cracked black pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Blend soaked cashews, water, lemon juice, garlic, and salt in high-powered blender until completely smooth, 1-2 minutes. Scrape down sides as needed.
2. Transfer to bowl. Stir in diced cucumber, dill, olive oil, and pepper.
3. Taste and adjust lemon/salt.
4. Store in jar in refrigerator. Stir before using.

**Use in:** Mediterranean bowls, Greek salad, mezze plate, grain bowls, as a dip

---

#### Chipotle Cashew Crema

**Yield:** ~1.5 cups | **Storage:** 7 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup raw cashews, soaked in boiling water 15+ minutes, drained
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
- 1-2 chipotle peppers in adobo (2 for more heat)
- 1 tbsp adobo sauce from can
- 1 clove garlic
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin

**Instructions:**
1. Add all ingredients to high-powered blender.
2. Blend until completely smooth, 1-2 minutes.
3. Taste and adjust lime/salt/heat. Add more chipotle for spicier.
4. Store in squeeze bottle or jar.

**Use in:** Tacos, burrito bowls, taco salad, stuffed sweet potatoes, drizzle on everything Mexican

---

#### Lemon-Tahini Dressing

**Yield:** ~1 cup | **Storage:** 10 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 1/4 cup tahini, well stirred
- 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 small clove garlic, minced or pressed
- 1/4 tsp kosher salt
- 3-4 tbsp water (to desired consistency)
- Freshly cracked black pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Whisk together tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, maple syrup, garlic, salt, and pepper.
2. Add water 1 tbsp at a time, whisking, until you reach pourable dressing consistency.
3. Taste and adjust lemon/salt.
4. Store in jar. Shake before using. If it thickens, add a splash of water.

**Use in:** Grain bowls, salads, Mediterranean dishes, drizzle on roasted vegetables

### Roasted Vegetables

#### Mediterranean Roasted Vegetables

**Yield:** ~6 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 2 medium zucchini, cut into half-moons
- 2 bell peppers (any color), cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 red onion, cut into wedges
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes
- 4 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 4 cloves garlic, smashed

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Toss all vegetables with olive oil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread on 1-2 sheet pans in single layer (don't overcrowd).
4. Roast 25-30 minutes, stirring halfway, until tender and caramelized.
5. Cool and store.

**Use in:** Mediterranean bowls, mezze plate, grain bowls, stuffed in pitas

---

#### Fajita Vegetables

**Yield:** ~4 cups | **Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 3 bell peppers (mixed colors), sliced into strips
- 1 large yellow onion, sliced into strips
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- Juice of 1 lime

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. Toss peppers and onions with oil and all spices.
3. Spread on sheet pan in single layer.
4. Roast 20-25 minutes until tender and slightly charred.
5. Squeeze lime juice over top, toss.
6. Cool and store.

**Use in:** Fajita bowls, tacos, burrito bowls, quesadillas

### Breakfast Components

#### Overnight Oats Base (Makes 3 servings)

**Storage:** 5 days refrigerated

**Base per jar:**
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup oat milk
- 1/4 cup coconut yogurt
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- Pinch of salt

**Flavor Variations:**

**Jar 1 - Chocolate Cherry:**
Add to base: 1 tbsp cocoa powder, 2 tbsp dried cherries, 1 tbsp almond butter

**Jar 2 - Apple Cinnamon:**
Add to base: 1/2 diced apple, 1/2 tsp cinnamon, 1 tbsp chopped walnuts, extra drizzle maple syrup

**Jar 3 - Tropical:**
Add to base: 2 tbsp frozen mango (thaws overnight), 1 tbsp shredded coconut, 1 tbsp pepitas

**Instructions:**
1. Add base ingredients to jar, stir well.
2. Add flavor mix-ins, stir.
3. Seal and refrigerate overnight (or at least 4 hours).
4. Eat cold or microwave 1-2 minutes.

#### Chickpea Scramble Base

**Yield:** 4 servings | **Storage:** 4 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 medium yellow onion, diced
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 tsp ground turmeric
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
- 2 tbsp water
- Spinach (add fresh when reheating)

**Instructions:**
1. Roughly mash chickpeas with fork or potato masher (leave some texture).
2. Heat oil in skillet over medium. Cook onion and pepper until soft, 5 minutes.
3. Add garlic, cook 1 minute.
4. Add mashed chickpeas and all spices. Stir well.
5. Add nutritional yeast and water. Cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
6. Cool and store in portions.

**To serve:** Reheat portion in skillet, add handful of spinach, stir until wilted. Serve with avocado, hot sauce, and toast.

### Snacks

#### Classic Hummus

**Yield:** ~2.5 cups | **Storage:** 7 days refrigerated

**Ingredients:**
- 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained and rinsed (reserve 1/4 cup liquid)
- 1/3 cup tahini
- 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 3-4 tbsp reserved chickpea liquid or water
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Paprika for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. Add tahini, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and cumin to food processor. Process 1 minute until smooth.
2. Add chickpeas. Process 2 minutes, scraping down sides.
3. With processor running, stream in chickpea liquid and olive oil until very smooth.
4. Taste and adjust lemon/salt.
5. Store in container. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle paprika when serving.

---

#### Peanut Butter Energy Balls

**Yield:** 16 balls | **Storage:** 2 weeks refrigerated, 2 months frozen

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
- 1/3 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips (vegan)
- 2 tbsp chia seeds
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

**Instructions:**
1. Mix all ingredients in bowl until well combined.
2. Refrigerate 15 minutes if too sticky.
3. Roll into 16 balls (about 1.5 tbsp each).
4. Store in container in fridge.

---

#### Spiced Roasted Chickpeas

**Yield:** ~2 cups | **Storage:** 5 days in airtight container at room temperature

**Ingredients:**
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained, rinsed, and dried VERY well
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Pat chickpeas VERY dry with paper towels (this is key for crispiness).
3. Toss with olive oil.
4. Spread on sheet pan in single layer.
5. Roast 35-40 minutes, shaking pan every 10 minutes, until golden and crispy.
6. Immediately toss with spices and salt while hot.
7. Let cool completely before storing. They'll crisp up more as they cool.

# Emergency Meals

Didn't prep? Here's what you can throw together in 10 minutes:

**Chickpea Salad Sandwich:** Mash canned chickpeas with vegan mayo, mustard, diced celery, salt, pepper. Serve on bread with lettuce.

**Bean & Rice Bowl:** Canned beans + microwave rice + salsa + avocado + hot sauce.

**Pasta Aglio e Olio:** Cook pasta, toss with olive oil, garlic, red pepper flakes, salt. Add white beans for protein.

**Avocado Toast Deluxe:** Toast + mashed avocado + canned chickpeas + everything bagel seasoning + hot sauce.

**Hummus Wrap:** Spread hummus on tortilla, add whatever raw veg you have, roll up.
