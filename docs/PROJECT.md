# Matt's Veganuary 2026 Meal Plan

## Project Overview

A comprehensive 31-day plant-based meal plan for January 2026, providing:
- 3 meals per day (breakfast, lunch, dinner)
- Daily snacks
- Weekly grocery lists
- Meal prep instructions
- Step-by-step recipes

**Dietary Restrictions:**
- 100% plant-based (vegan)
- NO tofu or soy products (no edamame, tempeh, soy milk, soy sauce, etc.)
- Serving size: 1 adult male
- No other restrictions (gluten, nuts, etc. all fine)

**Preferences:**
- Spice level: Any (mild to hot all welcome)
- Cuisine variety: Mix it up (Mexican, Indian, Mediterranean, Asian, etc.)
- Cooking style: Heavy weekend prep, minimal weekday assembly (5-15 min)
- Equipment: High-powered blender, food processor, Instant Pot, slow cooker

---

## Document Structure

Following the Rainbow Plant Life format, each week will include:

### Phase 1: Weekly Overview
- This week's meals at a glance
- Daily meal schedule (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner / Snacks)

### Phase 2: Grocery List
- Consolidated list by category (Produce, Pantry, Refrigerated, Spices)
- List by recipe
- Grocery notes and substitutions
- Time-saving shortcuts

### Phase 3: Meal Prep Guide
- Weekend prep instructions (60-90 min)
- Component prep (sauces, dressings, bases)
- Vegetable prep (washing, chopping, storing)

### Phase 4: Recipes
- Full recipes with ingredients and instructions
- Recipe notes, tips, and storage
- Estimated active/inactive cook times

### Phase 5: Appendix
- Emergency meal ideas
- Nutrition information (optional)

## Soy-Free Substitutions Reference

| Instead of... | Use... |
|---------------|--------|
| Soy sauce | Coconut aminos |
| Soy milk | Oat milk, almond milk, coconut milk |
| Tofu | Chickpeas, white beans, seitan (if gluten-ok), jackfruit |
| Tempeh | Lentils, black beans, mushrooms |
| Edamame | Lima beans, fava beans, peas |
| Soy-based yogurt | Coconut yogurt, oat yogurt, almond yogurt |
| Soy-based cheese | Cashew-based cheese, nutritional yeast |
